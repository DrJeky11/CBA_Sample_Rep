The ASTACSOP Aviation series Naval Tactics, Techniques, and Prodecures (NTTP) Tactical Pocket Guide is prepared under the direction of the Commander, Naval Aviation Warfare Development Center (NAWDC), and approved by the Commanding Officer, Marine Aviation Weap -ons and Tactics Squadron One (MAWTS-1). This pocket guide standadizes tactical procedures for rotary-wing and tiltrotor missions in support of the Marine Air Ground Task Force (MAGTF). NTTP 3-22.5-ASTACSOP, Tactical Pocket Guide—ASTACSOP, should be used in conjunction with all Marine Corps rotory-wing and tiltrotor aircraft NTTP 3-22.1 Tactical Employment and 3-22.3 Aircraft Fundamentals manuals.
